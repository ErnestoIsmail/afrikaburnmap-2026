
This is how it works: everything you see in Tankwa Town - including all the artworks, theme camps, mutant vehicles, and associated items - has been made by people just like you, who have worked their sweet asses off to create something special.

Operating times are listed as given, but like everything at AfrikaBurn, theme camps have everchanging schedules. Where camps have not listed specific activity times, please feel free to pop by and ask (politely) when they'd like you to participate with them. Even if times are provided, don't expect things to run like clockwork.

Check the individual listings, and at the camps themselves for specific opening hours. Everyone has a different idea of what family friendly (FF) is, so please double-check with camps first.
