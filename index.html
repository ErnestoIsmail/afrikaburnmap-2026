About this interactive map

This interactive map is built from the official AfrikaBurn 2026 site map and the 2026 WTF Guide, with additional linking and offline-friendly navigation layered on top.

Developed by Chris Laidler from the .

The original interactive map project was created by Bernard Bravenboer from .

This is an independent helper tool. There are no guarantees, and some information may still be incomplete or incorrect, but care has been taken to make it as accurate and useful as possible. Heading information is only as accurate as the phone; a mis-calibrated compass can lead to inaccurate heading display.
